The rails for the internet of markets

Founded by Bram Cohen, the inventor of BitTorrent, Chia is a next-generation, open-source blockchain that has been built from the ground up to enable a vision of globally accessible, interconnected markets, increasing their robustness and reducing compliance overhead. Building on tried-and-tested technologies and peer-reviewed, award-winning research in cryptography and computer science, our platform is uniquely secure and designed for sustainability, compliance and programmability.

Sustainable and secure Circular Drive Initiative

As a founding member of the Circular Drive Initiative, we join William McDonough and the computer hardware industry in our commitment to reduce e-waste by enabling the secure reuse of storage devices. We support the opportunity to securely reduce e-waste by giving hard disk and solid-state drives a second life via Chia farming, the process of earning rewards for validating transactions in the Chia network. The Chia netspace amounts to over 40 exabytes, with over 350,000 nodes to date.

Compliant

Chia believes that in order to deliver on the promise of the next generation of blockchains, we must be regulatory compliant. It is our stated goal to become a public reporting company subject to the Securities Act of 1933. Chia's native currency, XCH or chia, has never been sold by Chia Network for fiat currency, and we believe it will be regulated as a commodity by the CFTC.

Sustainable

With its innovative Proof Of Space and Time consensus, Chia uses 0.16% of the annual energy consumption of Bitcoin, and 0.36% of Ethereum's. Chia is part of the Circular Drive Initiative that reduces e-waste through the secure reuse of storage devices, promoting the second use of hard drives for Chia farming.
