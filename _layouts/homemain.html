Sustainable and secure Circular Drive Initiative

As a founding member of the Circular Drive Initiative, we join William McDonough and the industry in declaring a commitment to reduce e-waste by supporting the secure reuse of storage hardware. Chia netspace represents over 40 exabytes with over 350,000 nodes. We support the opportunity to securely reduce e-waste by giving drives a second use in Chia farming.
