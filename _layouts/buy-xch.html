Buy XCH

Chia Network has partnered with Stably and Prime Trust to create an option for you to convert your USD into USDS, the first stablecoin on the Chia blockchain.

Now anyone with USDS can use offers with the Chia light wallet to buy XCH. Instead of needing to go through a centralized exchange to obtain XCH, you now have the cheaper, decentralized, option of using a trustless peer-to-peer exchange of USDS for XCH using Offers.

How to Buy XCH

Follow the 4 steps below to buy XCH with your USD fiat currency:

1. Open a Stably Prime Trust account

  • Sign up for a Stably account
  • You will need to provide your name, email address, phone number

2. Fund your Stably account by depositing USD to your Stably Prime account

  • Go through Stably’s Know-Your-Customer (KYC) process to complete setting up your account (may take up to a day or two to complete ). After the KYC process has completed, you can initiate a “Deposit” by transferring from a traditional bank through supported wire transfer protocols (e.g., SWIFT, ACH).

3. Withdraw USDS directly to your Chia light wallet

  • Note that the ability to withdraw USDS to your Chia light wallet will begin on January 17, 2022.
    1. Download and install the Chia light wallet
    2. In the light wallet go to +Add Token and select "Stably USD"
    3. From your Stably USD account, click on Withdraw → Stablecoins → USDS
    4. Add your USDS Chia wallet in Stably and then specify the amount of USDS to complete your withdrawal

4. Post or redeem an offer to trade USDS for XCH

  • Post an offer or monitor the offers being posted at Keybase - chia_offers, Hashgreen, or Offerbin.

Stably and Prime Trust

Stably USD (symbol: USDS) is a one-to-one U.S. Dollar backed, redeemable stablecoin. U.S. Dollar collateral is held in FDIC-insured trust accounts managed by Prime Trust. USDS is Stably's inaugural stablecoin.

Stably Prime (by Prime Trust) is a self-directed custodial account offered by Prime Trust through Stably.
